Bacon Egg and Cheese

Description

Whether you get yours at a deli, from a coffee shop, or just make it at home, the bacon, egg, and cheese sandwich checks all the boxes: Protein, fat, carbs. Salty, rich, gooey, crispy. And above all, a good BEC is deeply satisfyingâ€”a welcome cure for everything from a hangover to heartbreak.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • American Cheese
  • Roll of Bread
  • Butter
  • Optional: Condiments

Steps

  1. Toast the roll
  2. Add three strips of bacon to the pan and cook until golden brown
  3. As the bacon completes cooking, add two whipped eggs to another pan
  4. As the eggs near completion (fluffy yellow), add a slice of American cheese on top
  5. Assemble completed sandwich, and add favorite condiments
